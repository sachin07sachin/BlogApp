1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, specifically:

  • Your name and email address.
  • The content you create (blog posts and comments).
  • Your communications with us (contact forms).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages.
  • Send you the "Daily Digest" of unread activity (if you have opted in).
  • Monitor and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in our database. We use industry-standard encryption (hashing) for passwords. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Choices

You may update or correct your account information at any time by logging into your account settings. You may also unsubscribe from our Daily Digest emails by clicking the link in the email footer.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact Page.
