We want Blog App to be a safe and welcoming place for everyone. Please help us keep it that way.

The Do's

  • Be Respectful: Treat others with kindness. Healthy debate is encouraged, but personal attacks are not.
  • Share Knowledge: Use your posts to teach, inspire, or entertain.
  • Report Issues: If you see something that violates these rules, please let us know.

The Don'ts

  • Hate Speech: We have zero tolerance for hate speech, bullying, or harassment.
  • Spam: Do not use the platform purely for self-promotion or link farming.
  • Illegal Content: Do not post content that violates any laws or infringes on copyrights.

Enforcement

If you violate these guidelines, we may take action including:

  • Removing your content (posts or comments).
  • Issuing a warning to your account.
  • Suspending or permanently banning your account.
